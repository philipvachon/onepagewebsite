About Me

My name is Philip. I want to live in a world with more artists who know how to communicate their vision. I'm helping to make that a reality by working in the music industry in marketing and by running PHILIP branding & design. I design logos, clarify brands, and code websites. I can also be found writing songs, drawing portraits, or reading about existentialism.

I am a graduate of the NYU Undergraduate Music Business Program and have years of experience in the music industry. I have interned at companies like Red Light Management, Glassnote Records, and Columbia Records. To get more information on my work experience, check out my resumé below.

My Process

My design process focuses first and foremost on clarifying identity and vision. I design websites and logos for artists and creative people who feel that who they are is too important to be conveyed by a template. Design thinking has helped me build artists' brands as well as create marketing materials for organizations.
